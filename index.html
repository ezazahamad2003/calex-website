How Calex streamlines approvals

1. Call or Upload

Citizens and developers initiate contact through phone, chat, or document upload. Calex immediately begins processing the inquiry using natural language understanding.

2. Retrieve Rules

Our AI instantly accesses relevant municipal codes, zoning regulations, building requirements, and policy documents. Real-time rule retrieval ensures current compliance standards.

3. Analyze & Cite

Calex analyzes requests against regulatory frameworks, identifies compliance issues, and provides specific citations. Every recommendation includes verifiable source documentation.

4. Human Review

Pre-screened, compliant submissions reach human reviewers with comprehensive analysis. Staff focus on decision-making rather than initial compliance checking.

5. Export & Track

Generate complete audit trails, export decisions with supporting documentation, and track approval status. Full transparency throughout the entire process.
